About : Internship

The experience during the internship was an experience that I will never forget because it all happened without being planned properly. I and two other friends chose to do an internship at Uitm Seri Iskandar even though it is far from our home. At that time, it was a challenge because our state was implementing PKP and was in the red zone. I was apprehensive but still continued the steps to get there to undergo an internship. At first, I was very worried and unsure of my decision, but when the internship period was over, I found it very difficult to leave the place. Many unexpected things happened and gave me a thousand memories.

Momment & Activities

Shoes sole

My shoes sole were torn off on the first day of internship. We entered the library for only 10 minutes, then broke back to college to change into other shoes and came back to the library. When we told to the librarian there, they laughed very hard at our situation at that time.

Fixed the book

Me and my friends had the opportunity to learn how to repair books, and we were able to repair the books ourselves. At first, we made a lot of mistakes but after 2 or 3 books, we managed to fix it neatly and beautifully.

Poster

The librarian gave us the task of making a poster, for use during registration to the IT room. We had to make this poster twice because the first one has a lot of mistakes. This is the result!

Shelf Displacement

This day was so much fun because we were allowed to wear sports shirts. Almost 15 to 20 shelves or maybe more, which we managed to settle right in half a day!.



Library Staff

This library staff is very knowledgeable. He always talks about knowledgeable things even if we only meet for a moment. A lot of new things we know from him!

Library Staff

There are many stories about this library staff. She was a person who helped us a lot in this internship process. From beginning to end, she was the one who always helped us, even outside of working hours. She is like our own family...

Friends

This internship has brought us closer. We only met in two semester, and it was sometimes because we were still new. After the internship, I think we already know each other's temperament because all the things there we do together.

Farewell Ceremony

The last day we were at the library as internship students, the library staff and librarians there held a farewell ceremony and gave us certificates and memories photo. Everyone was very nice, friendly and generous, made us very comfortable there.
